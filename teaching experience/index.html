Varun Venkatesh Teaching experience

During my PhD I found a love for teaching. I held numerous roles as a teaching assistant in a number of biology courses. These roles included responsibilities ranging from the leading of labs, development of teaching labs and the co-ordination of courses. My teaching experience and skills has culminated in being offered two short term lecturing fellowship contracts in early 2017 at Victoria University of Wellington. In the future I hope to progress my teaching skills and design more integrative and engaging learning experiences for students.
